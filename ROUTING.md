# ROUTING.md — @aikdna/content_strategy

## When this domain SHOULD be loaded

- Evaluating whether a content idea is worth pursuing
- Judging if a topic has real audience tension
- Assessing content quality beyond surface metrics
- Deciding editorial priorities and content investment
- Diagnosing why content underperformed

## When this domain should NOT be loaded

- Writing content from scratch (generation, not strategy)
- Scheduling or managing content calendars (operations, not judgment)
- SEO keyword research (data analysis, not content judgment)
- Formatting or designing content layout (design, not strategy)

## Easily confused tasks (contamination risks)

| Task that looks relevant | Why it should NOT load this domain |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| "Write a blog post about X" | Content creation, not strategy |
| "What keywords should I target?" | SEO research, not content judgment |
| "Schedule our content for next month" | Operations, not strategy |
| "Design a landing page layout" | Visual design, not content strategy |

## Recommended test statements

**Should load:**
1. "Is this content idea worth investing in, or is it just noise?"
2. "Why did this article perform poorly despite good SEO?"
3. "Which of these three topics should we prioritize for Q3?"

**Should skip:**
1. "Write a 1000-word article about sustainable fashion"
2. "Find keywords for our fitness app blog"
3. "Create a content calendar template"

## Confidence guidance

- Load without asking when: task is about content evaluation, prioritization, or diagnosis
- Always skip when: task is creation, scheduling, or SEO mechanics
